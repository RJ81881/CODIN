Precedence and Assosciativity of Operators

Precedence means when in an expression we have more than one Operator then which operation has to be performed first is decided by the precedence rule.

Assosciativity specifies that when we have the same operators occuring more than once in an expression then from where we need to start our computation like : either L to R or R to L

The naming of an address is known as variable.and Variable is the name of memory location.

Precedence Operators Assosciativity
1 () , [] , . , -> L to R
2 ++ , -- , ~ , ! R to L
3 * , / , % L to R
4 + , - L to R
5 << , >> L to R
6 < , <= , > , >= L to R
7 == , != L to R
8 & , ^ , | L to R
9 && , || L to R
